OUR STORY

Two brothers and a lifelong dream to open their own food business in Hawaii. This dream started to become a reality when Gio and Javi the owners of Guadalajara Grill moved to Hawaii. They started working at the same restaurant where Javi, worked as a Chef. Gio was the Manager and with both of their experience they used it to start their very own business.

FAMILY OWNED AND OPERATED SINCE 2016

Since 2016 Javi and Gio have been great companions. Influencing each other to make the right choices in order to achieve their dream.
